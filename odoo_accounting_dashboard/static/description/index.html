Frequently Asked Questions

What is the purpose of this app?

This app helps users monitor financial performance through interactive accounting dashboards, charts, and payment analysis reports.

How does the app help in financial analysis?

The app provides income, expense, profit, receivable, and payable charts, enabling users to analyze financial data and make informed decisions.

Can I track customer and vendor payments?

Yes, the app includes dedicated customer and vendor payment lists, along with aged receivable and payable analysis for better payment tracking.
